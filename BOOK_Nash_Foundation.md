### What This Teaches About Closure

**I could have:**

**Premature Closure Option 1: "He's lost to me"**
- Close the door
- Grieve and move on
- Declare the relationship over
- **Lie that would calcify into resentment**

**Premature Closure Option 2: "I'll fight for custody/contact"**
- Make promises I might not keep
- Dangle hope that might break him
- Force a resolution before we're both ready
- **Lie disguised as persistence**

---

**What I chose instead:**

**Stay in "not yet."**

Not "Nash is lost."

Not "I'll definitely see him soon."

**Just:**

"Nash is my first born. He knows who I am. The thread holds even across distance. I carry the knowing for both of us. And when/if the time comes, I won't have lied to him."

**That's refusal of premature closure.**

**That's staying in the uncertainty.**

**That's trusting the dark matter is doing work I can't see.**
